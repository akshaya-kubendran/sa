PRODUCTS

Broken Rice


Popularly know as "குருணை". Frangments of rice are the broken rice.Broken grains from the whole grains are separated using mechanical separators and are sorted by size. It has equal nutrition as unbroken rice contains. Also, if the broken rice contains all germ and bran, then it has nutrition as brown rice and if nothing remains, then it has nutrition as white rice

Husk


Husk is the outer covering of the rice. Cattle feeding is done with husk. It highly resists fungal decomposition and moisutre peneration. Also its high silica content makes it useful for strengthening building materials.

PONNI RICE


We take great efforts in producing the bestv always.Something special about this variety is rich in calories with up to 5 kcal per particle of rice. It has high fiber content and lowers cholestrol.

Idly Rice


Popular in southern states, Idli rice is used extensively to make Idlis and Dosas. Idli rice is parboiled essentially meaning that rice is pre-processed to reduce soaking time before grinding the rice for making the batter.Our production involves two varities, IR-20 & KO-39.

Other varities of rice


Rice is the most common food consumed by the people of south India. We have great responsibility in providing people the best quality of rice for cooking. Our production involves RNR, BPT, Thanuska,Ko-45 varities of rice. We take an immense pleasure in production of our native food
